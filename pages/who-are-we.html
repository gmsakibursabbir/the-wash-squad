Who We Are

The Wash Squad was acquired in 2013 by Matthew Norman. Our reputation in the pressure washing industry dates back over a decade. First contributing in online forums to keeping up to date through social media and other social platforms. During that time, we’ve traveled the nation attending round tables in Myrtle Beach South Carolina, Texas, and San Diego California. We learned about what it truly takes to own and operate a successful exterior cleaning business. Our company is eco-friendly and uses professional grade, custom designed equipment. No matter the job size we can handle very large jobs to smaller jobs for residential owners. We try and treat every customer as if they are important to us. Contact our company today to enhance your curb appeal.

The Wash Squad is a family-owned company that operates in Colorado. For more than ten years now, we have been active in the cleaning service industry. This gives us a great deal of experience with customers and enhances the level of service we provide for our own customers. We will always charge you fair prices for the extraordinarily high-quality services you purchase from us.

Founded on a promise to provide each customer with reliable and friendly pressure washing services, we work hard to be the top pressure washing company in our area. We have earned a reputation for being the best choice in Colorado for you if you need pressure washing services for your home or business. Our professional staff is constantly being trained to keep up with the latest advances in technology and pressure washing techniques as well as to keep our knowledge of the pressure washing industry at its peak.

We believe in being environmentally responsible when it comes to the way we do our job. Every pressure washing job is completed using detergents and soaps that won’t harm the environment. Our staff has been both trained and certified to comply with the Clean Water Act policies set forth by the Environmental Protection Agency. We only use cleaning products that are safe for your home or business.

Customers who call us for a pressure washing job can get an on-site estimate for free. In addition, we also perform services such as cleaning roofs and gutters, removing graffiti and rust. We work hard to provide services throughout the greater Denver Metro area. We provide each customer with the best and friendliest service that it is possible to offer.
